Methodology

Built from BLS records, then annotated with LLM-scored exposure.

This is a research product, not a deterministic forecast. The goal is to make the shape of the market legible, then let you inspect the assumptions in the open.

Pipeline

One static site, generated from a reproducible Python pipeline.

  • Scrape and cache occupation pages from the Bureau of Labor Statistics Occupational Outlook Handbook.
  • Parse pages into structured summaries, compensation, education, and outlook fields.
  • Score exposure with an LLM rubric focused on digital task substitution and workflow reshaping.
  • Rebuild the static site payload and ship production updates on a fixed Vercel URL.
